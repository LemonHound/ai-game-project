How to Play Tic Tac Toe

🎯 Objective: Get three X's in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)

🎮 Your Turn: Click any empty square to place your X

🤖 AI Turn: The AI will automatically place its O

🏆 Winning: First to get three in a row wins!

🔄 Draw: If the board fills up with no winner, it's a tie
